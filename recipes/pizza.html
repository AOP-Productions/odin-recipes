Mexican Pizza

Description

Let's Go Over the Steps to Make Homemade Pizza, this recipe was taken from Allrecipes While some of the toppings maybe different then regular pizza, but I promise that these changes are worth it.

Step-by-Step

While this dish can seem daunting with all of the ingredients, but if you take your time and follow these steps. You will be enjoying this meal in no time.

  1. Cook the beef with onions and garlic, drain, and season.
  2. Place a tortilla on each pie plate and top with refried beans.
  3. Cover with the beef, top with another tortilla, and bake until the tortillas are crisp.
  4. Layer the toppings in this order; Salsa, cheeses, tomatoes, green onions, and jalapeno.
  5. Bake until the cheese is melted. Cool, then cut each pizza into four slices.

Ingredients

You need to be precise with these servings as to not make the tasteful flavors and toppings work extremely well with each other. Proceed at your own risk.

  • 1/2 Pound of Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Onion, Diced
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • 1 tablespoon Chili Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Ground Cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon Paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) can of Refried Beans
  • 4 (10 inch) Flour Tortillas
  • 1/2 Cup of Salsa
  • 1 Cup of Shredded Cheddr Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
  • 2 Green Onions, Chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 Cup of Thinly Sliced Jalapeno Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup of Sour Cream (optional)
