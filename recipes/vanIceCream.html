Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

A Classic Dessert Option, you can never go wrong with the basics. Allrecipes.com A Great Place to get many recipes for many different meals.

Ingredients

You may think making Vanilla Ice Cream is hard, but it only takes 4 easy ingredients, have a pen and paper ready.

  • 2 Cups of Heavy Cream
  • 2 Cups of Half & Half Cream
  • 3/4 Cup of White Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon of Vanilla Extract

Follow these 2 Easy Steps

Not only do you have 4 ingredients, you only have to worry 2 Easy Steps and you can create this simple yet elegant dessert. Whenever You Want.

Step 1
Whisk heavy cream and half-and-half together in a large bowl. Gradually whisk in sugar until blended, then whisk in vanilla. Cover and refrigerate until very cold, at least 3 hours or up to 3 days.

Step 2
Remove from the refrigerator and whisk mixture to blend. Pour into an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions, about 20 minutes. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
